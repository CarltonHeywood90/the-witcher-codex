# Boreas Mun: An Archival Study

While the hunt for the Elder Blood involved grand sorcery and royal armies, its success on the ground depended entirely on men like **Boreas Mun**. A veteran woodsman, professional guide, and premier wilderness tracker, Mun was recruited into Stefan Skellen's elite special commando unit due to his unmatched capacity to read the wilderness. He is not a sadistic killer like Bonhart or a political schemer like Skellen; Mun is a practical, deeply pragmatic frontiersman who views tracking as an exact science where the land never lies.

## Hunting across the Steppes

Mun’s primary operational value was his capacity to maintain a hot trail across the most hostile, featureless terrains of the southern provinces. When Ciri broke away from her captors and fled into the massive, windswept wilderness of the Korath borderlands, standard military scouting units lost her trail within hours.

Mun, however, was able to read the microscopic disruptions in the environment—a bent blade of dry grass, the specific compression of sand, the scent of a horse's sweat on an old trail. He successfully guided Skellen's heavily armed mercenary vanguard across miles of hostile territory, keeping the pressure on the fleeing girl until she was driven toward the frozen wastes of the North. For Mun, the hunt was a matter of professional pride and contractual obligation, completely detached from the apocalyptic weight of Ciri's heritage.

> "A sorceress can hide her face with an illusion, and a spy can lie with every breath. But a horse cannot step on stone without leaving dust, and a human cannot run through the brush without leaving a piece of their cloak. The land remembers everything."
> — Instruction to Skellen's scouts

## The Pivot of Conscience

As the campaign progressed and Skellen's methods became increasingly unhinged and desperate, Mun experienced a profound internal crisis. The systematic cruelty unleashed by Leo Bonhart and the bloodthirsty nature of the mercenaries began to conflict with his basic frontiersman code of honor. 

When the hunt culminated in a chaotic battle on the frozen surface of Tarn Mira, Mun realized he was fighting on the side of monsters. He broke away from Skellen's command, utilizing his wilderness skills to assist the surviving targets and ultimately transitioning from an imperial hunter into a crucial asset for those trying to survive the absolute wrath of the secret service.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Boreas Mun is an elite survivalist and environmental analyst:

* **Micro-Environmental Tracking:** Exceptional capacity to identify, date, and follow footprints and environmental disturbances across stone, sand, and frozen mud.
* **Wilderness Logistics Management:** Master of navigating hostile, unmapped territories, securing water sources, and maintaining speed during long-range cavalry pursuits.
* **Frontier Survival Arts:** Highly skilled in weather prediction, natural medicine, and maintaining high operational efficiency in sub-zero or desert environments.
