# Ida Emean aep Sivney: An Archival Study

To understand the full depth of the Lodge's intellectual reach, one must look beyond human magic entirely to the ancient elven Sages—the **Aen Saevherne**. **Ida Emean aep Sivney** is the absolute embodiment of this primeval power. An elven seer from the remote, misty heights of the Blue Mountains, Ida Emean possesses classic, ethereal elven features, magnificent amber-red hair, and an inscrutable, detached demeanor. She does not practice the standard, aggressive elemental chaos utilized by human mages; instead, she manipulates the ancient, fundamental pathways of time, prophecy, and genetic memory.

## The Neutrality of the Sage

Ida Emean was brought into the Lodge by Francesca Findabair to serve as an independent, non-aligned counterweight to the human factions. Unlike the human sorceresses who are deeply entangled in the rise and fall of kingdoms, Ida operates on a macro-historical timeline. She views the entire human race as a temporary, chaotic blip on the face of the Continent, participating in the Lodge's shadow games solely because she recognizes that the fate of the entire world hinges on the genetic potential of Ciri's ancient lineage.

As an *Aen Saevherne*, Ida is bound by ancient laws of neutrality and hidden knowledge. She communicates primarily in riddles, ancient metaphors, and warnings derived from the legendary Prophecy of Ithlinne. Her presence in the Lodge provided the definitive roadmap for decoding the mutations of the **Hen Ichaer** (the Elder Blood), ensuring the sorceresses understood the cataclysmic, world-ending stakes involved if Ciri's power were to be weaponized incorrectly by secular rulers.

> "You see a girl who can inherit a kingdom, or a weapon to break an empire. I see the pivot upon which the White Frost turns. If her bloodline fails, the frost will swallow this world, and it will matter very little who wore the crowns in Tretogor or Nilfgaard."
> — Address to the closed council of the Lodge

## The Hidden Scholar

Despite her immense power, Ida Emean avoids open conflict whenever possible. She maintains a completely hidden, isolationist existence when not actively consulting with Francesca or the Lodge, drawing her power from the deep, untouched ley lines of the mountains. Her mastery of ancient elven optics and long-range scrying allows her to monitor continental shifts without ever leaving her sanctuary, making her an untraceable, invaluable informational asset to the elven nationalist cause.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Ida Emean represents a tier of ancient, non-human magical mastery that completely transcends standard human capability:

* **Prophetic Stream Decoding:** Capable of reading the shifting currents of destiny and prophecy, identifying critical historical pivot points and the true intent behind genetic lineages.
* **Ancient Elven Elixir Alchemy:** Masters the creation of legendary, long-lost elven draughts capable of reversing advanced necrotic curses and accelerating cellular regeneration beyond human capabilities.
* **Aen Seidhe Glamour and Illusion Weaving:** Highly proficient in deploying permanent, reality-bending illusions that seamlessly conceal entire mountain passes or ancient elven structures from human detection.
