# The Professor: An Archival Study

In the criminal underworld of the Northern Kingdoms, contract killers are typically brutish thugs or desperate cutthroats. **Ralf Blunden**, universally known as **The Professor**, completely subverts this expectation. Well-educated, highly articulate, and instantly recognizable by his distinctive wire-rimmed spectacles and academic vocabulary, the Professor operates as a high-tier mercenary and independent hitman who treats assassination with the cold, detached precision of a university lecture.

## The Academic Contract

The Professor’s unique reputation stems from his absolute professionalism. He does not kill out of passion, malice, or ideological loyalty; he treats every contract as a strict business arrangement requiring detailed planning, tactical reconnaissance, and clean execution. His high fees are justified by his complete reliability and his ability to operate within high-society environments where standard thugs would be instantly exposed.

His path crossed with major players when he was hired by criminal syndicates and rogue political factions to eliminate targets that were interfering with regional trade networks. Using his educated background to gain access to secure municipal archives and noble estates, he meticulously maps out his targets' daily routines, environmental vulnerabilities, and security guards before launching swift, surgical strikes that leave local city watches completely baffled.

> "Violence, when properly executed, is not an emotional outburst. It is a mathematical equation where the target's life is subtracted through the application of precise kinetic force. The glasses? They simply ensure my percentages remain absolute."
> — Dialogue recorded in an Anchor tavern

## The Collision with the White Wolf

Despite his intellectual arrogance and meticulous preparation, the Professor’s career met its absolute boundary when his contracts brought him into direct conflict with Geralt of Rivia. Treating a Witcher as a standard human target was his definitive tactical error. 

While his planning allowed him to orchestrate complex traps and utilize specialized toxins designed to disable standard guards, he consistently underestimated the non-linear combat adaptability, mutated reflex speed, and raw battlefield instinct of a professional monster hunter. His clinical approach to murder ultimately shattered against the chaotic, fluid violence of the Witcher’s blade, serving as a warning that academic theory cannot replace mutated survival instinct.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

The Professor operates as a hyper-calculating tactical assassin and urban tracker:

* **Surgical Precision Strike Profile:** Specializes in close-quarters thrusts with light, high-grade steel blades designed to pierce weak points in leather or chain mail armor.
* **Tactical Reconnaissance Analysis:** Exceptional ability to read architectural blueprints, map out patrol paths, and exploit structural blind spots in fortified compounds.
* **High-Society Integration:** Utilizes his advanced vocabulary, impeccable manners, and educated background to pass as a scholar, clerk, or noble administrator within restricted court zones.
