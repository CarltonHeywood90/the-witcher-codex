# Keira Metz: An Archival Study

If Philippa Eilhart represents the heights of political ruthlessness, **Keira Metz** represents the pragmatic desire for luxury and absolute self-preservation. Small in stature, quick-witted, and possessing long, pale-blonde hair and a signature ankh amulet, Keira served alongside Triss Merigold as a trusted advisor to King Foltest of Temeria. Despite her preference for opulent court life, expensive perfumes, and high society comfort, Keira is an exceptionally capable, combat-tested sorceress whose vanity hides a sharp, survivor-centric intellect.

## From Courtly Splendor to the Mud of Velen

Keira’s fortunes collapsed overnight during the coup on Thanedd Island. Caught completely off guard by the violent fighting between Northern loyalists and Nilfgaardian sympathizers, she was physically thrown out of a high window by the massive renegade wizard Artaud Terranova, breaking her leg and leaving her badly bruised.

Her situation worsened drastically when King Radovid V initiated his brutal, state-sponsored witch hunts. Stripped of her royal titles and hunted by the Witch Hunters, the comfort-loving sorceress was forced into a miserable, underground exile in the swampy, monster-infested backwaters of **Velen**. Living incognito as a local village "witch," she traded her high-end cosmetics for raw herbal remedies, a demeaning existence that fueled a desperate, reckless obsession with securing a pardon from Radovid at any cost.

> "I was meant for silk sheets and ballrooms, Geralt. Not scavenging for roots in a swamp while the peasants whisper about my teeth. There isn't a spell in the world that can wash the smell of this mud out of my skin."
> — Dialogue during an investigation in Velen

## The Research of Alexander

During her desperate stay in Velen, Keira discovered that the abandoned tower on Fyke Isle held the experimental journals of the deceased mage Alexander. These papers contained invaluable, catastrophic research regarding the **Catriona plague**—a devastating, highly contagious hemorrhagic disease tearing through the Continent. 

Keira sought to secure these files to use them as an absolute bargaining chip, intending to offer the cure to King Radovid in exchange for her safety and the restoration of her luxurious lifestyle. Her short-sighted willingness to hand weaponized biological data over to a paranoid, genocidal monarch underscored her primary defining flaw: when cornered, Keira will gladly gamble with the fate of the world to save her own skin.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Keira operates with a highly functional, defensive, and utility-driven magical toolkit:

* **High-Frequency Telekinetic Shockwaves:** Specializes in generating defensive kinetic barriers and precision telekinetic bursts to throw back armored attackers.
* **Advanced Illusionary Disguises:** Highly skilled in weaving massive, area-of-effect visual distortions, concealing entire laboratories or cave networks behind standard stone facades.
* **Micro-Biological Neutralization:** Possesses a deep, academic understanding of epidemiology and cellular purification, making her highly efficient at treating complex infections and neutralizing venoms.
