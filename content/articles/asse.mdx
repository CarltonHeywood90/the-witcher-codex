# Asse: An Archival Study

While other members of the Rats sought luxury, freedom, or escape from their pasts, **Asse** was driven by a single, unyielding obsession: absolute vengeance. The son of a simple country blacksmith, Asse's life was permanently derailed when a detachment of Nilfgaardian vanguard cavalry swept through his village during a routine pacification campaign. His entire family was systematically butchered, and his home was burned to ash. Joining Giselher's command shortly thereafter, Asse became the grim, quiet executioner of the gang, viewing every raid against imperial interests as a small payment on an unpayable debt of blood.

## The Hammer of Retribution

Asse brought a raw, physical strength to the Rats that balanced the agile, speed-focused combat styles of Mistle and Kayleigh. Having spent his youth swinging a heavy smithing hammer, he wielded a massive, broad-bladed short sword with devastating kinetic force, capable of shattering iron shields and cleaving through heavy leather vestments.

Within the gang's dynamic, Asse was traditionally the most taciturn and brooding. He showed complete indifference to the fine silks and expensive perfumes that obsessed Giselher and Kayleigh, choosing instead to spend his portion of their loot on high-grade whetstones, weapon maintenance, and upgrading his warhorse. He was the primary driver behind the gang's increasingly aggressive attacks on official Nilfgaardian supply lines and tax collectors, pushing the group from simple highway robbery into the dangerous territory of active political subversion.

> "My father spent thirty years forging tools for peasants and clearing horse hooves. The black coats burned him alive in his own furnace just to watch the grease smoke. Every time my blade cuts through a Nilfgaardian cloak, I hear the hammer hitting the anvil."
> — Dialogue recorded during a camp night near the Riedbrune swamps

## The Wall of Iron

Asse's single-minded focus on physical retribution made him an incredibly reliable vanguard fighter during ambush operations, but it also made him fatally predictable. He lacked the tactical flexibility required to deal with elite, non-traditional combatants. 

When Leo Bonhart cornered the Rats in Jealousy, Asse was the first to charge the bounty hunter directly, attempting to overwhelm him with pure, brute physical strength. This direct approach proved disastrous; Bonhart effortlessly sidestepped the heavy, linear downward swing, utilizing Asse's own forward momentum to deliver a clean, lethal thrust that instantly neutralized the gang's strongest physical asset.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Asse operates as a heavy vanguard shock-fighter and weapon smith:

* **High-Kinetic Short-Sword Combat:** Utilizes exceptional upper-body strength to deliver crushing, unparriable downward strikes designed to break an opponent's guard.
* **Field Forge and Armor Maintenance:** Acts as the gang's internal armorer, repairing damaged blades, shoeing horses, and customizing stolen protective gear in remote wilderness camps.
* **Anti-Cavalry Ambush Mechanics:** Specialized in constructing simple, effective wilderness traps (such as caltrops and hidden pits) to disable pursuing military horses.
