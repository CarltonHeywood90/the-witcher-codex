# Ihuarraquax: An Archival Study

Among the most mysterious beings to touch the life of the Child of the Elder Blood is **Ihuarraquax**, a young, pure-white unicorn of the Aen Seidhe. While the legends of the Continent often depict unicorns as either mythical chimeras or simple beasts, Ihuarraquax is an intelligent, highly sentient entity from a dimension parallel to the Witcher’s own. Ciri famously dubbed him "Little Horse" after finding him trapped and poisoned in the Korath Desert, and the bond they formed during her survival ordeal remains one of the few moments of pure, untainted connection in her entire saga.

## The Healer in the Sand

When Ciri discovered the young unicorn in the arid wastes of the Korath Desert, he was near death, struck down by a sand-monster’s venom. Defying the explicit warnings of her mentor Yennefer, Ciri channeled the raw, chaotic energy of fire magic—a power that nearly destroyed her own mind—to purge the poison and save his life. This act of profound selflessness created a telepathic link between them that transcended speech. Ihuarraquax, recognizing in Ciri a "being" rather than a simple human, became her silent guide and protector through the shifting dunes.

> "I will not let you die here, in this dust. You are the only thing in this world that doesn't want to use me, doesn't want to hunt me, and doesn't want to carve me up for parts. You are my friend, Little Horse. That is enough."
> — Ciri, during the crossing of the Korath Desert

## Guardian of the Dimensions

As Ciri’s journey evolved from survival to destiny, Ihuarraquax reappeared at pivotal moments to anchor her crumbling sanity. He served as her guardian against the predatory reach of Eredin and the Wild Hunt, utilizing his own innate command over dimensional rifts to shield her. In the final movements of the legend, it was Ihuarraquax who assisted Ciri in the transport of the mortally wounded Geralt and Yennefer to the Isle of Avallac'h, fulfilling his duty as a guardian of the Elder Blood before vanishing into the mists, forever returning to the lands beyond the reach of human conflict.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Ihuarraquax operates as a cosmic anchor and guardian entity:

* **Innate Dimensional Manipulation:** Possesses the ability to stabilize space-time, create safe passages through inter-dimensional voids, and mask signatures from magical trackers.
* **Pure Elemental Healing:** Can channel primordial energies to neutralize magical toxins and accelerate organic cellular regeneration.
* **Telepathic Resonance:** Capable of establishing non-verbal, high-fidelity consciousness links with entities of the Elder Blood, providing guidance and warning without the risk of linguistic misinterpretation.
