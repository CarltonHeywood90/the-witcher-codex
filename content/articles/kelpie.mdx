# Kelpie: An Archival Study

In a life defined by displacement and loss, the magnificent black mare Ciri named **Kelpie** became the only constant, physical symbol of her freedom. Originally the property of a man named Hotspurn, the horse was never given a formal name by her former master, who dismissed the practice as "silly." When Ciri first laid eyes on her, crossing a river with an almost supernatural grace, she saw the creature in the shifting water and immediately dubbed her "Kelpie"—an naming choice that reportedly deeply amused the reclusive scholar Vysogota of Corvo.

## The Bond of the Path

Kelpie was more than a beast of burden; she was a combat-hardened survivor, possessing a temperament as fierce and uncompromising as Ciri’s own. The mare could be summoned from vast distances via a magical bracelet, allowing Ciri to navigate the treacherous borderlands of Geso and the southern territories without being tethered to any single settlement. She carried Ciri through the most violent chapters of her life, including the flight from the Rats and the desperate race toward the frozen lakes of Tarn Mira.

> "She doesn't need a bridle, Vysogota. She knows where the trail ends, and she knows that as long as she runs, they can't catch us. She is the only thing I have ever owned that I didn't have to steal or kill for."
> — Ciri, discussing her bond with the mare

## Tactical & Combat Capabilities

Kelpie is a high-performance equine asset tailored for the rigors of the Path:

* **Superior Endurance and Speed:** Capable of sustained gallop cycles over extreme distances, even when navigating the rugged terrain of the Blue Mountains or the soft mud of the Pereplut Swamps.
* **Combat-Hardened Temperament:** Uniquely conditioned to remain calm under the pressure of magical detonations, high-intensity sword combat, and the terror-inducing presence of the Wild Hunt.
* **Magical Linkage:** Possesses a semi-attuned reaction to Ciri’s magical bracelet, allowing for near-instantaneous emergency transit to her rider’s location.
