# Houvenaghel: An Archival Study

While the armies of Nilfgaard and the North fought for grand ideals on the battlefields, individuals like **Houvenaghel** built vast financial empires on the raw suffering left in their wake. A wealthy, highly influential merchant based in the town of Claremont near the border of Cidaris, Houvenaghel is a cousin to the notorious Leo Bonhart. He is a pure sociopath wrapped in the silks of a respectable trader, utilizing his immense wealth, local political corruption, and family ties to operate the most brutal, illegal gladiatorial arena on the Continent.

## The Mechanics of the Claremont Arena

Houvenaghel’s primary source of wealth and local power was the **Claremont Arena**—a sunken, blood-soaked amphitheater where captive slaves, non-humans, captured soldiers, and wild monsters were forced to fight to the death for the entertainment of decadent nobles and bloodthirsty locals. Houvenaghel treated this slaughter strictly as a commercial asset, maximizing his profits through heavy gambling rings, ticket sales, and the trafficking of illegal captives.

His operation reached a dark zenith when Leo Bonhart brought him a captured, badly wounded Ciri. Recognizing the immense novelty and gambling potential of forcing a young girl with Witcher training to fight in his pit, Houvenaghel enthusiastically integrated her into his schedule. He orchestrated a series of grotesque spectacles, arming Ciri with a light sword and forcing her to butcher waves of heavily armed gladiators and wild beasts while he collected massive payouts from the elite citizens betting on her survival.

> "Everything in this world is a transaction, my dear cousin. A king trades his peasants for land, a soldier trades his blood for a coin, and I trade the screaming in my pit for gold. And let me tell you—the screaming pays exceptionally well."
> — Conversation with Leo Bonhart regarding the arena schedules

## The Infrastructure of Cruelty

Houvenaghel's success was sustained by his deep integration into the local economic elite. He maintained complete immunity from regional laws by providing heavy bribes to local prefects, hosting private, exclusive combat spectacles for corrupt politicians, and managing a highly complex supply chain of beast-trappers and slave-traders. His estate was a monument to hypocritical luxury—a beautiful, peaceful villa filled with fine art and expensive wines, built directly adjacent to the iron cages and breeding pens where his gladiators were starved and beaten into compliance.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Houvenaghel operates as a supreme commercial monopolist and syndicate leader:

* **Gladiatorial Entertainment Infrastructure:** Owns and maintains a fully fortified, private combat arena with specialized beast containment cells and secure high-capacity seating blocks.
* **Trans-Continental Black Market Supply Chain:** Direct logistics links to illegal slave rings, rogue beast-catchers, and high-level corrupt political figures across multiple provinces.
* **Monetary Syndicate Control:** Manages massive capital reserves and deep gambling syndicates, allowing him to easily finance private mercenary garrisons to secure his estate.
