# Milva: An Archival Study

Among the exceptional individuals who comprised Geralt of Rivia’s fellowship, **Milva** (born Maria Barring) provided the raw, practical survival foundation that kept the group alive. An elite human archer who found sanctuary within the forbidden, matriarchal forest of Brokilon, she exists as a rare bridge between the bitter non-human resistance and the human world. Free of political ambitions or magical alignments, her presence in the Hanza was defined by razor-sharp pragmatism and an unmatched mastery of the longbow.

## Daughter of the Forest

Raised in Upper Sodden, Maria fled into the ancient woods of Brokilon to escape domestic trauma, where her preternatural talent for stealth and archery earned her the respect of the insular, human-hating Dryads. Given the name *Milva* (meaning "The Kite" in the Elder Speech), she became a vital asset to Queen Eithné.

During the Second Nilfgaardian War, Milva operated as a high-stakes guide for the **Scoia'tael**, leading shattered elven commando units through imperial lines into the safety of the dryad sanctuaries. She knew every hidden spring, animal trail, and lethal choke point in the southern valley, living a life entirely detached from human civilization until she was tasked with nursing a broken, post-Thanedd Geralt back to health.

> "I don't know much about prophecies, Elder Blood, or kings. But I know how to track a blood trail in a blizzard, and I know that if I release this string, that soldier across the river dies before he can blink."
> — Recorded during the trek across the Yaruga

## The Heart of the Fellowship

Milva initially joined Geralt’s quest out of a sense of profound debt and reluctant empathy, but she rapidly evolved into the tactical spine of the Hanza. While Geralt brooded and Jaskier provided theatrical distractions, Milva secured food, scouted enemy movements, and maintained absolute situational awareness.

Her journey was marked by deep personal sacrifice. Discovering she was pregnant early in the trek, she chose to push forward through brutal warzones rather than abandon the fellowship, viewing the proxy family of the Hanza as her true home. Her blunt, unrefined honesty cut through the philosophical debates of Regis and the defensive posturing of Cahir, forcing the disparate group to function as a cohesive, deadly unit.

## Combat Metrics

Milva’s lethality is entirely mechanical and biological, representing the pinnacle of non-magical human martial skill:

* **Brokilon Longbow Mastery:** Employs a massive, heavy-draw reflex bow capable of piercing plate armor or dropping a moving target at distances exceeding two hundred paces.
* **Wilderness Camouflage:** Capable of rendering herself completely invisible in standard forest or alpine terrain, moving without triggering the sensory warnings of wildlife or scouts.
* **Guerilla Reconnaissance:** Master of tracking, counter-tracking, and rapid ambush deployment, routinely outmaneuvering elite imperial vanguard scouts.

Milva stands as a tragic, brilliant example of pure fidelity—a woman of the earth who left the safety of her ancient woods to fight through a burning continent for a cause that wasn't her own.
