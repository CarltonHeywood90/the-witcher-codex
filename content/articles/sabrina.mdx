# Sabrina Glevissig: An Archival Study

If Sheala de Tancarville represents the absolute quiet of a laboratory, **Sabrina Glevissig** represents the roar of a battlefield. The chief advisor to King Henselt of Kaedwen, Sabrina was a proud, loud, and predatory sorceress who wore her classic Kaedwenuan beauty like a weapon. Famous for her volatile temper, her sharp tongue, and her preference for plunging necklines and daring courtly fashion, she did not merely advise King Henselt—she dominated his court, using raw intimidation and public displays of arcane terror to keep the stubborn monarch perfectly aligned with her personal goals.

## The Fire of Lormark

Sabrina's strategic value to Kaedwen was absolute, but her relationship with King Henselt was a toxic, power-hungry struggle. Her darkest historical moment occurred during the brutal **Battle of Lormark**, a massive border conflict where Henselt’s forces were locked in a meat-grinder trench war against the army of Aedirn.

Realizing that Henselt's forces were on the verge of losing the strategic valley—a failure that would completely ruin the Lodge's regional territorial plans—Sabrina chose to completely bypass the king's command structure. She ordered her battle mages to unleash a massive, forbidden rain of meteoric fire directly onto the battlefield. The apocalyptic spell made no distinction between friend or foe, instantly vaporizing thousands of Aedirn soldiers alongside thousands of Henselt’s own loyal Kaedwenuan infantrymen. While the catastrophic strike successfully halted the battle, her callous disregard for Kaedwenuan blood sealed her domestic fate.

> "Henselt thinks he rules because he wears a crown of iron and screams at his generals. He is a child throwing a tantrum in a sandbox. The real boundaries of this kingdom are drawn in fire, and I am the one holding the torch."
> — Private journal entry found at Ard Carraigh

## The Execution and the Curse

Furious at her absolute betrayal and the slaughter of his men, a vengeful King Henselt immediately ordered her arrest. Sabrina was stripped of her arcane focuses, subjected to a brief military trial, and sentenced to be burned alive at the stake on the very battlefield she had incinerated.

As the executioners lit the pyre, Sabrina utilized her final, agonizing moments of life to weave a massive, blood-fueled death curse. Her agonizing screams infused the soil of the Pontar Valley with dark energy, trapping the souls of the fallen soldiers in a permanent, supernatural battlefield fog. For three years, the valley remained an active, haunted warzone where spectral armies re-enacted the slaughter every single day, a terrifying monument to the absolute, vengeful spite of a dying sorceress.

## Tactical and Combat Capabilities

Sabrina was a premier frontline combat mage specializing in wide-area devastation and psychological dominance:

* **Mass-Scale Pyrotechnic Bombardment:** Exceptional at channeling massive, meteoric fire storms capable of wiping out entire infantry regiments simultaneously.
* **Aggressive Courtly Intimidation:** Highly skilled at utilizing raw charisma, structural blackmail, and sudden displays of localized kinetic power to dominate stubborn political rulers.
* **Blood-Bound Death Curses:** Possesses the rare, high-tier capability to anchor permanent, multi-year reality distortions to specific geographic zones using her own life force.
