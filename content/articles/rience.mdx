# Rience: An Archival Study

Every grand mastermind requires a blunt, vicious instrument to handle the filthy work of tracking, interrogation, and assassination. For Vilgefortz, that instrument was **Rience**. A disgraced former student of the Ban Ard Academy who was expelled for petty theft and moral degeneracy, Rience was recruited by the master mage due to his complete lack of human empathy and his natural affinity for raw, destructive pyromancy. He is a cruel, deeply insecure sadist who delights in utilizing his magical gifts to terrorize those who cannot fight back.

## The Hunt for the White Wolf's Cub

Rience was unleashed upon the Northern Kingdoms with a single directive: track down any individual who had contact with Ciri following the fall of Cintra. Operating with a massive black budget provided by Vilgefortz, Rience organized a network of low-life informants, thugs, and professional killers to squeeze information out of the northern underground.

His methods were defined by utter brutality. He captured the bard Dandelion in a dark alley, subjecting him to horrific, systematic physical torture to extract details regarding Ciri's location at Kaer Morhen. His operation was only halted by the sudden, lethal intervention of Yennefer of Vengerberg. During the chaotic arcane exchange that followed, Yennefer countered his pyrotechnic streams, turning his own fire directly back onto his face. The resulting magical burn permanently scarred his features, leaving him with a twisted, distorted mask of scar tissue that fueled his obsessive, lifelong hatred for the sorceress and her allies.

> "Tell me where the girl is, singer. Tell me, or I will peel the skin from your fingers an inch at a time, and then I will use my spark to ensure you never sing another note for the rest of your short, painful life."
> — Interrogation of Dandelion

## The Ice of Tarn Mira

Rience's hunt eventually brought him into a direct, fatal confrontation with Ciri on the frozen surface of Tarn Mira. Armed with modern tracking equipment and backed by Stefan Skellen’s heavily armed mercenary force, Rience believed he had finally cornered his prize.

However, he failed to account for the environmental terrain and Ciri's lethal combination of Witcher agility and ancestral agility. Donning ice skates, Ciri utilized the thick lake mist to transform the battlefield into a horror sequence for her pursuers, slicing through the mercenaries one by one. As the ice fractured beneath the weight of the combatants, Rience fell through into the sub-zero water. Clinging desperately to the jagged edge of the ice sheet, his freezing hands were completely systematically severed by Ciri's skates, sending the pyromancer screaming down into the dark, icy depths of the lake below.

## Tactical and Combat Capabilities

Rience operates as a mid-tier combat mage specializing in volatile offensive tactics:

* **Concentrated Pyrotechnic Stream Manipulation:** Focuses on launching high-temperature, continuous jets of flame designed to breach wooden fortifications and incinerate organic targets.
* **Underground Information Network Coordination:** Highly skilled at bribing municipal guards, hiring mercenary cutthroats, and manipulating criminal syndicates across the North.
* **Basic Portal Matrix Navigation:** Capable of utilizing pre-linked teleportation keys provided by Vilgefortz to execute quick escapes from hostile zones.
