# Francesca Findabair: An Archival Study

Universally recognized as the most beautiful woman on the Continent, **Francesca Findabair** (known in the Elder Speech as *Enid an Gleanna*, or the Daisy of the Valleys) is a monumental figure whose political weight matches her ancient lineage. An Aen Seidhe elven sorceress of immense age and power, Francesca is the pure ideological heart of elven nationalism. Unlike her human peers who play at advisory roles, Francesca successfully leveraged continental chaos to secure an official sovereign elven state, cementing her position as the first Queen of Dol Blathanna.

## The Deal with the Sun

Francesca’s ultimate priority has always been the preservation and restoration of the dying elven race. During the First and Second Nilfgaardian Wars, she recognized a crucial opportunity in the imperial ambitions of Emperor Emhyr var Emreis. She entered into a dangerous, calculated alliance with the Southern Empire, orchestrating the betrayal of the human Northern Coalition during the bloody coup on Thanedd Island.

In exchange for her deep-cover subversion of the Brotherhood of Sorcerers, Emhyr granted her absolute sovereignty over **Dol Blathanna** (the Valley of Flowers), establishing it as an official elven kingdom under imperial protection. However, this sovereignty carried a devastating, bloody price tag: Emhyr forced her to officially disavow and abandon the *Scoia'tael*—the desperate young elven guerrilla fighters who had bled for her cause in the northern forests. Francesca made the agonizing, cold-blooded decision to sacrifice her people's youth to ensure the survival of the elven race's cultural and political core.

> "To survive an empire, you do not throw your children at their pikes. You wait for the monster to grow hungry, and you trade them something they want for the dirt beneath your feet."
> — Secret correspondence to the elven vanguard

## Core Member of the Secret Lodge

Despite her deep imperial ties, Francesca was a primary founder of the Lodge of Sorceresses alongside Philippa Eilhart. She understood that a true magical matriarchy could transcend the brutal racial boundaries between humans and elves. 

It was Francesca who initially identified the immense geopolitical and genetic value of Ciri’s bloodline, revealing to the Lodge the ancient secrets of the **Hen Ichaer** (the Elder Blood). Her ancient repository of elven genealogical records became the baseline roadmap the Lodge used to trace Ciri's lineage, aiming to control her future offspring to reshape the destiny of the world.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Francesca utilizes ancient, non-human magical traditions that fundamentally differ from human applications:

* **Ancient Aen Seidhe Elven Sorcery:** Masters primeval elven incantations that draw directly from nature faults, rendering her immune to standard anti-magic dimeritium fields for brief durations.
* **Mass-Scale Jade Compression:** Highly proficient in high-tier artifact compression, capable of instantly shrinking living targets (such as Yennefer during the Thanedd coup) into miniature jade statuettes for long-term transport.
* **Centuries of Geopolitical Insight:** Possesses deep generational knowledge of continental bloodlines, ancient treaties, and territorial histories dating back before the Conjunction of the Spheres.
