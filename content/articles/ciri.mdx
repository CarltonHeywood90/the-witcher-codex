# Cirilla Fiona Elen Riannon: An Archival Study

Few individuals on The Continent possess a lineage so fraught with geopolitical consequence and cosmic terror as **Cirilla Fiona Elen Riannon**, known universally as Ciri. As the sole princess of the fallen kingdom of Cintra and the last bearer of the unique, untainted **Elder Blood**, her existence represents the focal point around which the fate of empires and dimensions turns.

## The Shattered Kingdom

Ciri's early life was defined by the strict etiquettes of the court of Cintra, raised under the fierce, watchful eye of her grandmother, Queen Calanthe. However, her royal childhood came to a cataclysmic end during the **Slaughter of Cintra**, when the forces of the Nilfgaardian Empire breached the capital city's walls. 

Fleeing through the burning streets as a child, orphan to a massacred dynasty, Ciri wandered the wilderness of Sodden until fate intervened. She was discovered by **Geralt of Rivia**, the Witcher to whom she had been bound years prior via the absolute mystical decree of the **Law of Surprise**. 

## A Dual Education

Geralt brought the traumatized princess to the relative safety of the School of the Wolf’s mountain keep, **Kaer Morhen**. Here, her unconventional training began. While she never underwent the chemical mutations of the Trial of the Grasses, she was subjected to the grueling physical, blindfolded sword-training regimes of the witchers. She consumed specialized alchemical herbs that accelerated her muscle growth, transforming her into a lethal, agile martial combatant.

Her education expanded dramatically when the sorceress **Yennefer of Vengerberg** took custody of her. Yennefer recognized that Ciri was a *Source*—an individual with an innate, volatile connection to primal magical energy. Under Yennefer's strict, deeply maternal guidance at the Temple of Melitele, Ciri learned to channel, govern, and respect the terrifying currents of magic sleeping within her genetics.

> "I can run across worlds, jump through centuries, and alter the paths of stars. But all I ever truly sought was a home where my presence didn't bring fire down upon those I love."
> — Recovered quote from the Child of Promise

## The Lady of Space and Time

The true nature of Ciri’s power stems from her identity as the descendant of the elven mage Lara Dorren. This **Elder Blood** grant she possesses allows her to manipulate the very fabric of reality. She is the *Zireael* (Swallow), the figure central to Ithlinne’s prophecy, which foretells an individual capable of saving the world from a localized, planetary ice age—or destroying it completely.

Because her genes allow her to tear through dimensional boundaries and teleport across space and time effortlessly, she became the target of every major superpower on the Continent. She was hunted relentlessly by her own biological father, Emperor Emhyr var Emreis, the dark sorcerer Vilgefortz, and the otherworldly spectral riders known as the **Wild Hunt**. 

Ciri’s journey is a violent odyssey of survival. She is a princess stripped of a throne, a witcher who never took the mutations, and a mage whose power dwarfs the entire Brotherhood of Sorcerers—a living bridge between the mortal realm and the infinite cosmos.
