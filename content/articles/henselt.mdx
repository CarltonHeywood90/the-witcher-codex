# King Henselt: An Archival Study

If the Northern Kingdoms had a singular ruler who embodied raw, uncompromising expansionist greed and stubborn resilience, it was **King Henselt** of Kaedwen. Ruling from the cold, stone-carved capital of Ard Carraigh, Henselt was a loud, aggressive, and deeply suspicious monarch who wore a thick beard and a perpetual scowl. He viewed the world entirely through the lens of territorial conquest, possessing a fanatical belief that Kaedwen was destined to absorb its neighbors and dictate the terms of northern survival.

## The Vulture of the Pontar Valley

Henselt was a highly capable military commander, but his political morality was entirely transactional. His defining obsession was the **Pontar Valley** (specifically the strategic territory of Lormark), an area he relentlessly tried to seize from neighboring Aedirn, leading to decades of bloody, cyclical border wars.

His darkest historical hour came during the Second Nilfgaardian War. As the imperial armies smashed through Aedirn's defenses, Henselt—instead of immediately reinforcing his northern ally—signed a secret, separate non-aggression pact with Emperor Emhyr var Emreis. This treacherous maneuver allowed Henselt to march his forces into upper Aedirn under the guise of an occupation, effectively cannibalizing his own ally's territory while the rest of the North burned. It was only when he realized Nilfgaard would inevitably betray him that he reversed course, deploying his elite Kaedwenuan heavy infantry to help hold the line at the Battle of Brenna.

## The Court of Iron and Hatred

Henselt’s domestic rule was notoriously brutal. He harbored a deep, vicious hatred for non-humans, actively financing human supremacist militias and unleashing total state pogroms against the elven and dwarven populations of his cities. This hyper-aggressive stance made Kaedwen the primary breeding ground for the *Scoia'tael* guerrilla uprisings, turning his own forests into permanent, blood-soaked warzones.

His court was dominated by heavy-handed military executioners and highly ambitious mages like **Dethmold**, a ruthless sorcerer who used dark magic and public executions to suppress any whispers of internal rebellion. Henselt’s absolute reliance on raw terror kept his kingdom unified from the outside, but it left the internal foundations of Kaedwen highly volatile and ripe for conspiracy.

## Tactical and State Assets

Henselt backed his imperial ambitions with the largest standing army in the Northern Realms:

* **Massed Heavy Infantry Formations:** Exceptionally skilled at deploying dense, iron-clad shield walls capable of weathering prolonged cavalry charges and heavy bombardment.
* **Ruthless Internal Counter-Insurgency:** Utilizes state-sponsored terror networks and martial law to systematically eliminate non-human resistance factions.
* **Adverse Winter Maneuvering:** Optimizes his military supply lines and troop movements to operate at peak efficiency across freezing, mountainous northern climates.
