# Reef: An Archival Study

While Giselher fled his military duties willingly, **Reef** represents the dark, discardable human waste of the imperial war machine. Originally a young conscript fighting under the banner of the Nilfgaardian Empire, Reef's world collapsed during a brutal, meat-grinder siege operation in the southern provinces. Severely wounded by enemy fire and callously left behind in the mud by his retreating commanders, he was discovered starving and bleeding out by Kayleigh. Nursed back to health by the outlaws, Reef shed his imperial loyalty entirely to become the quiet, intensely loyal vanguard anchor of the Rats.

## The Cast-Off Veteran

Reef’s primary characteristic within the gang's dynamic was a deep, quiet fatalism born from surviving his own state's abandonment. Having worn the black coat of Nilfgaard and witnessed the utter indifference of imperial officers toward their frontline infantry, he possessed an absolute hatred for state authority that equaled Giselher's. He adapted quickly to the gang's nomadic lifestyle, utilizing his formal military training to bring structured tactical discipline to their chaotic highway ambushes.

Though often quiet compared to the loud braggadocio of Kayleigh or the dramatic flair of Iskra, Reef was a terrifyingly efficient combatant. He favored standard-issue military weaponry, wielding heavy short swords and crossbows with the calm, methodical precision of a trained soldier. He viewed each successful raid against imperial tax collectors and military caravans not as a search for wealth, but as a direct, personal act of retribution against the Empire that had left him to die in the dirt.

> "The Empire tells you that you are a proud son of the sun right up until a halberd cracks your ribs and they leave you to rot in a ditch so the supply wagons can move faster. I don't serve empires anymore. I serve the people who pulled me out of the mud."
> — Conversation with Giselher regarding his past service

## The Final Stand

Reef's military conditioning made him highly reliable in a standard skirmish, but it left him structurally unequipped to handle a high-tier independent assassin. When Leo Bonhart cornered the Rats in the village of Jealousy, Reef attempted to establish a defensive holding line alongside Asse to protect the lighter skirmishers. 

Despite utilizing clean, disciplined military footwork and keeping his guard high, his baseline human reflexes were utterly systematically disassembled by the terrifying, fluid speed of the Witcher Killer. He fell alongside the brothers-in-arms who had saved his life, completing his journey from an abandoned imperial casualty to a fallen member of the Continent's most notorious outlaw pack.

## Tactical and Combat Capabilities

Reef operates as a disciplined frontline skirmisher and ranged support asset:

* **Standard Military Crossbow Proficiency:** Exceptional at maintaining high accuracy with heavy mechanical crossbows during high-stress cavalry pursuits.
* **Defensive Formational Tactics:** Brings formal infantry training to the gang, organizing effective defensive rings and parry structures when the group is cornered by regional guards.
* **Imperial Logistical Analysis:** Possesses detailed internal knowledge of Nilfgaardian military patrol schedules, supply wagon vulnerabilities, and provincial communication codes.
