# Eredin Bréacc Glas: An Archival Study

**Eredin Bréacc Glas**—the "Sparrowhawk"—is the lethal warlord of the Dearg Ruadhri, or the Red Riders. To the terrified peasantry of the Continent, he is the spectral lord of the Wild Hunt, a demonic wraith who descends during storms to harvest souls. To the reality of the Aen Elle, he is a calculating, ruthlessly ambitious commander desperate to secure a future for his dying people by any means necessary, including the exploitation of dimensional rifts and the enslavement of entire civilizations.

## The Red Rider's Burden

Unlike the mythic wraith portrayed in bedtime stories, Eredin is flesh and blood. He leads an elite elven cavalry unit from Tir ná Lia, a world suffering from the slow, agonizing depletion of its life-sustaining energy. His obsession with capturing Ciri is rooted in the unique genetic properties of her Elder Blood, which he believes is the only key capable of reopening the inter-dimensional paths required to evacuate his people from their crumbling reality and conquer the fertile, resource-rich lands of the human Continent.

Eredin’s leadership is defined by a cold, predatory pragmatism. When his predecessor, the Alder King Auberon, proved too indecisive to act against the encroaching threats, Eredin did not hesitate to usurp the throne. He is not a cartoon villain; he is a soldier who has abdicated every shred of traditional morality to become a force of nature, convinced that the survival of the Aen Elle justifies any level of cruelty, enslavement, or genocide.

> "Your world is a garden of weeds, and your people are but temporary custodians. The Elder Blood is the key to the gates, and once we turn it, the time for your petty kingdoms to exist will come to a final, permanent end."
> — Declaration during a portal raid

## Tactical and Combat Capabilities

Eredin serves as the primary offensive threat vector for the Aen Elle:

* **Inter-Dimensional Cavalry Tactics:** Master of high-speed, shock-and-awe raids utilizing portal-skipping cavalry units designed to destabilize regional defense perimeters.
* **Frost-Magic Weaponization:** Proficient in the application of lethal, magical cold-bursts and cryogenic weaponry, capable of neutralizing even highly resilient targets.
* **Spectral Psychological Warfare:** Expert at leveraging local folklore and superstitious terror to paralyze enemy morale before a formal assault even commences.
