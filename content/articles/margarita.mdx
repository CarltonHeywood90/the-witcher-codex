# Margarita Laux-Antille: An Archival Study

While most members of the Lodge of Sorceresses view magic as a crowbar to pry apart royal courts, **Margarita Laux-Antille** views it as a sacred institutional trust. The granddaughter of the legendary Iliona Laux-Antille, Rita succeeded the venerable Tissaia de Vries as the Rectoress of Aretuza, the premier academy for young magical talent on Thanedd Island. Tall, exceptionally beautiful, and possessing a famously warm yet authoritative presence, Rita stands apart from her peers by utterly rejecting personal political power in favor of an absolute, protective devotion to her students.

## The Shield of the Academy

Margarita's defining characteristic is her complete lack of political ambition. When Philippa Eilhart approached her to join the founding circle of the Lodge, Rita explicitly clarified that she was participating solely to secure an institutional shield for her academy and her girls. She viewed the petty rivalries of human kings with outright contempt, seeing them as brief, chaotic storms that threatened the timeless pursuit of academic and arcane excellence.

This single-minded devotion met its ultimate test during the chaotic northern witch hunts. As King Radovid V’s Witch Hunters attacked the magical communities, Aretuza was sacked and its students hunted down like animals. Rather than fleeing to protect her own skin like many other mages, Rita stayed behind to organize mass evacuations, personally fighting rearguard actions to buy her novices time to escape. She was eventually captured and thrown into the notorious Oxenfurt Prison, where she endured brutal conditions and systematic deprivation without revealing the hidden locations of her surviving students.

> "I don't care about your borders, your crowns, or your empires, Philippa. My only concern is the girls who come to Thanedd with raw power in their hands and fear in their eyes. If your Lodge can keep them from being burned at the stake, I am in. If not, don't waste my time."
> — Argument during the second session of the Lodge

## The Bond of Friendship

Rita shares a deep, fiercely loyal friendship with Tiss Merigold and Yennefer of Vengerberg, often serving as the emotional anchor when their personal lives and romantic entanglements with Geralt of Rivia threaten to overwhelm them. Her sharp wit and earthy, unapologetic sense of humor provide a rare breath of fresh air within the typically tense, backstabbing atmosphere of the sorceress sisterhood.

When Geralt set out to rescue her from the depths of the Oxenfurt containment cells, he found a woman who, despite being physically weakened by anti-magic dimeritium shackles, remained completely unbroken in spirit. Her immediate priority upon liberation was not her own recovery, but the immediate regrouping of the remaining mages to turn the tide against their tormentors.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Margarita is an elite academic theorist capable of deploying flawless, high-precision structural magic:

* **High-Grade Kinetic Barrier Matrix:** Specialized in weaving complex, immovable defensive shields capable of absorbing sustained artillery bombardment or high-tier offensive curses.
* **Academic Arcane Diagnostics:** Possesses an unmatched capacity to analyze, deconstruct, and neutralize foreign spell structures in real-time, completely dismantling enemy casting vectors.
* **Mass-Scale Extraction Portals:** Expert at calculating and maintaining highly stable, multi-passenger rifts designed to transport large groups of non-combatants out of active warzones safely.
