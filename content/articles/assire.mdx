# Assire var Anahid: An Archival Study

While the sorceresses of the Northern Kingdoms flaunt their power openly in glittering ballrooms, **Assire var Anahid** of Nilfgaard operated in an environment where visibility was a death sentence. Based in the southern province of Vicovaro, Assire is a thin, severe, and deeply bookish mage who traditionally eschewed the heavy makeup, rich perfumes, and revealing necklines of her northern peers. She is a supreme master of astrology, void scrying, and long-range structural observation, executing her duties with a quiet, clinical precision that kept her alive within the paranoid, anti-magic administrative apparatus of the Southern Empire.

## The Secret Envoy of the South

In the Nilfgaardian Empire, mages are viewed by the crown as functional state assets rather than political partners. When Francesca Findabair and Philippa Eilhart reached out to establish an independent, global sisterhood, Assire was selected as the vital, deep-cover representative for the Nilfgaardian factions alongside Fringilla Vigo.

Assire took massive personal risks to attend the secret sessions of the Lodge. To bypass the intense scrutiny of the imperial secret police and the suspicious eyes of Emperor Emhyr var Emreis, she had to utilize complex, highly illegal long-range astral projections. She quickly realized that the Lodge provided her with an invaluable counter-weight to the absolute authority of the imperial throne, giving her a secret venue to shape continental policy while presenting herself to her home government as a simple, detached scholar of the stars.

> "You northerners dress like courtesans and scream your power from the rooftops. In the south, if you want to keep your tongue, you learn to blend into the shadows of the library and let the kings think they thought of your ideas themselves."
> — Briefing to Fringilla Vigo

## The Tragedy of Imperial Oversight

Assire's ultimate loyalty lay with the preservation of her art and the sisterhood's potential to end the cyclical, bloody wars tearing the world apart. She spent months working within the Lodge to balance the aggressive, anti-Nilfgaardian biases of Philippa Eilhart, ensuring the southern perspective was factored into their long-term breeding plans for Ciri's bloodline.

However, operating a double-blind treason operation across imperial lines eventually caught up with her. During the chaotic political fallout of the Loc Muinne summit, her secret communications with the northern sorceresses were intercepted by the hyper-efficient imperial intelligence network. Viewed as an absolute traitor to the sun banner, Assire was summarily executed on the orders of the Emperor—a stark, brutal reminder that the shadow games of the Lodge carried the ultimate fatal consequence when played against an absolute ruler.

## Arcane Profiling & Assets

Assire represents the pinnacle of long-range, informational, and cosmic arcane disciplines:

* **Macro-Astrological Void Scrying:** Exceptional at tracking continental troop movements, predicting state collapses, and identifying specific targets across thousands of miles via stellar alignments.
* **Long-Distance Astral Projection:** Highly proficient in projecting perfect, sensory-complete phantoms of herself across continental boundaries, entirely bypassing physical border defenses.
* **Deep-Cover Data Suppression:** Expert at masking her own magical footprint, allowing her to host illegal communication channels directly beneath the noses of imperial inquisitors.
